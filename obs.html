What is the One Button Studio?

The One Button Studio (OBS) is a one-stop spot for all of your video recording needs. And, all you need is a USB Flash Drive (FAT 32). Cameras? Check. Lights? Check. Green and Blue Screen? Check!

The best part of it all? You can make these recordings with the press of just one button. So, what are you waiting for? Come in and record. After all, all it takes to make a professional-level recording is a USB Flash drive and a single button-push.

Resources

The One Button Studio has plenty of resources for students, staff and faculty at The College of Wooster to make high-quality recordings and videos. Such as...

  • A Mac Desktop Computer
  • Canon HD Video Camera
  • Chromatte Chromakey Screen
  • LiteRing Green/Blue Screen Toggle
  • WhiteBoard and EPSON projector

Whether you want to record yourself giving a presentation, film yourself in front of a green screen, or do an interview, the OBS will let you do so in style.

After you're done, you can take your recording outside to edit your film in iMovie, in the Digital Studio, and make your project shine. For anyone looking to record, there's no better place than the OBS!
